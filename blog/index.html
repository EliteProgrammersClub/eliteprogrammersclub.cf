Official Lunching of the Elite Programmers Club

Posted on November 4th, 2013

The Elite Progammers club which is found in the University Of Buea has officially been launched. This club is mostly an online community on Facebook and Google-Plus. We also have an IRC channel named #ublab hosted at Freenode (irc.freenode.net). Our main goal is to familiarize beginners with porgramming concepts and to improve on the programming skills of our members.
